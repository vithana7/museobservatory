## The question

*How do we imagine models of sustainable tourism for the Colli Tortonesi?*

That was the question handed to 33 people gathered at the Vinili e Vinelli festival in September 2024. They came from ten different countries. Fourteen were between 21 and 35. Eleven between 36 and 55. Eight between 56 and 70 and beyond. None of them had been asked to agree on anything. They had been asked to imagine together.

This was Horizon 001 — cocoex's first community-led campaign, and the first delivered Future Lab.

## The Future Lab

A Future Lab is not a workshop in the conventional sense. It draws from Robert Jungk's Zukunftswerkstatt methodology — a structured participatory process in which a community moves through phases of critique, utopia, and implementation. The point is not consensus. The point is to surface what a group of people can imagine when they are given time, structure, and the genuine freedom to speculate.

The Future Lab for Horizon 001 was hosted during Vinili e Vinelli, the annual music and wine festival in the Colli Tortonesi hills.

Memose Vithana participated as an embedded artist — present inside the process, not directing it.

## What the community proposed

Participants built towards the question of sustainable tourism — the kind that might serve the territory without consuming it. What emerged were proposals for slow-tourism routes connecting local producers, designed with intergenerational exchange at their centre: younger visitors alongside older residents, knowledge moving in both directions.

At the end of the Lab, the community voted on which NGO their campaign should support. They chose Slow Food Tortona.

## The report

cocoex produced a formal report on the Future Lab and delivered it to Slow Food Tortona. The organisation integrated the community's vision into their own plans — a rare and concrete outcome for a participatory process. The report did not sit on a shelf.

## What came after

Roughly half the Horizon 001 cohort reconvened at a cocoex event at Cantine Volpi the following summer, 2025. The community did not dissolve when the festival ended. That is, in the end, the point.

## Impact

The community's proposals reached Slow Food Tortona through a formal report. The organisation incorporated the vision into their planning.
